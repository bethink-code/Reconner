The cash gap

You're turning over good money. So why doesn't your bank account or pocket show it?

If your books balance and the money still doesn't show, this page is for you.

You're thinking

Yes, exactly. But my cash ups balance. How can money be missing if everything balances?

Here's the thing: balancing and certainty are not the same thing. Your cash ups can balance perfectly and you can still be losing money. A cash up confirms your numbers agree with each other. It cannot tell you whether the money that should exist actually does.

You're thinking

So the numbers agreeing with each other doesn't mean no one is taking anything.

Cash is where the tricks live. Reprints, under-rings, voids before the count, skimming. Your point of sale shows what should have happened. Your actual cash out shows what did. The gap between those is what you're feeling.

You're thinking

So I need to compare what the point of sale says I should have against what I actually took out. And that's why I feel like I'm missing money.

Exactly. We call it the cash gap. Lekana finds it and shows you where to look. Once you see it, you know where it's going.
