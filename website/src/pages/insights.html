Report 01

Reconciliation overview.

Report 02

Attendants.

Who is consistent. Who is slipping. The shortfall, allocated to the people who handled the transactions.

Total sales per attendant, matched amount, unmatched amount, declined transactions and the calibration error contribution. The pump calibration error is split out separately because that is not the attendant's fault. The rest is.

Report 03

Declined card transactions.

Every declined card, grouped by card number. The system flags the patterns that matter.

Repeated decline attempts in a short window. Late-night declines on the same card. Cards that decline and then mysteriously recover. The fraud patterns that hide in a transaction log become visible the moment the data is grouped properly.
