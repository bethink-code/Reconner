Data & Confidentiality Policy

What Lekana sees

Lekana only ever sees two things: your point-of-sale transaction records and your bank transactions. That’s it.

Not your full books. Not your accounting system. Not your margins, your suppliers, your payroll, or anything else that runs your business. Lekana sees what moved through your till and what settled in your account. Nothing more.

What Lekana does with your data

Lekana uses POS and bank transaction data to match transactions from your till against bank deposits, identify discrepancies (reconciliation), and detect patterns that indicate leakage (Insights). That’s the only purpose. No secondary use. No selling. No sharing.

During the pilot

Your POS and bank transaction data flows into Lekana’s system. Lekana reconciles them daily. You see all findings in real time. Your data remains your property at all times.

After the pilot

If you continue as a customer: your data stays to power your ongoing reconciliation. If you walk away: your data is permanently deleted within 7 days. Lekana does not retain, sell, analyse, or reuse your data for any other purpose.

Confidentiality of findings

Whatever Lekana finds in your business stays between us. If Lekana surfaces a useful result and you’re comfortable, we may share the headline outcome anonymously — no data, no specifics. Only with your explicit permission.

Your rights

  • You own your data. Always.
  • You can request deletion at any time.
  • You can see everything Lekana sees.
  • You can walk away at the end of the pilot with no obligation.

The business behind this
