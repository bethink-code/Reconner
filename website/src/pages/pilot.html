What this is

A short, close, honest pilot.

Three weeks per business

One week to onboard. Two weeks running.

A short, focused pilot. Long enough for the before-and-after to be real. Short enough that you know where you stand, fast.

Week 1 · Onboarding

Get the data flowing. Capture the before.

We get your sales data flowing in. We get your bank statements flowing in. We help you measure the "before" - the time you spend reconciling today, the leak you suspect, the gaps in confidence. We sit with you. We do not rush this part.

Weeks 2-3 · Running

lekana runs against your real operation.

Card, debtor and cash reconciliation. Every transaction matched, confirmed, or sent to Investigate. The Cash Gap report runs daily. You see what we see. We talk every week.

End of week 3 · Decide

Measure again. Then it's your call.

Same questions, new numbers. What the leak is. Where it came from. What was recovered. You see it first, and anyone else only with your permission. Then you decide: become a customer, or walk away. Your data stays yours either way.

Limits

One business at a time.

The pilot is open while we are still learning. We stay close to each business through onboarding and the two weeks running, which puts a natural limit on how many we can take at once. When we have what we need, we close it.

One site or many. We want businesses of different shapes in the pilot. A single-site business and a five-site group stress the platform differently, and we want to learn from both.
