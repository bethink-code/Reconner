What this is

A short, close, honest pilot.

The reconciliation engine works. What we need now is a wider set of businesses with different point-of-sale systems, different banks, different volumes, and different ways of working, so the platform is ready when we open it up.

That's the pilot. We onboard one business at a time. We stay close. We learn what breaks, what surprises us, and what your numbers tell us once everything is matched.

What we ask, what you get

One month. Both sides learn something.

What we ask of you

  • A real business on the platform. Real data, real banks, real cash.
  • Your starting point. The time you currently spend on reconciliation, the gaps in confidence, the things that don't add up. We help you put a number on it.
  • The full three weeks. One onboarding, two running.
  • Honest feedback. The good, the broken, the surprising.
  • If the pilot surfaces a useful result, permission to share the headline outcome — anonymously, and only if you're comfortable. No data. No specifics. Just what Lekana found and what it meant for your business.

What you get

  • The full platform, free, for three weeks.
  • Onboarding done properly. One week, not a Tuesday afternoon.
  • A direct line to Pieter and Garth. Not a ticket system.
  • Your data stays yours. Always.
  • The choice at the end. Continue as a customer, or walk away. No obligation either way.

Data and privacy

Your data is yours.

Lekana works with transactions. Nothing else. You choose what you share.

Lekana only ever sees two things: your point-of-sale transaction records and your bank transactions. That's it.

Not your full books. Not your accounting system. Not your margins, your suppliers, your payroll, or anything else that runs your business. Lekana sees what moved through your till and what settled in your account. Nothing more.

Lekana uses that to reconcile one against the other. Once the pilot is done, your data stays yours. Lekana does not retain it, sell it, or use it for anything beyond running your reconciliation.

Before the pilot starts, you sign an NDA. The details of what Lekana finds in your business stay between us.
