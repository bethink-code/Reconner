Common questions.

What does 'site' mean?

A site is one physical location. One forecourt, one shop, one cash up. A business may run one site or many. Above three sites, the per-site add-on applies.

Can I take Insights without Reconciliation?

No. Insights is built on the reconciled data. The base has to be there first.

Is there a setup fee?

No. There is no setup fee, no implementation charge, no minimum term. Month to month.

What happens if I take the pilot and decide not to continue?

You walk away. Your data stays yours. No obligation.
